1 Description

This task introduces PNG [4], a lossless image compression format used in this course for representing digital images and videos [2].

In our experiments, we are going to store a video as a sequence of files with names <prefix>000.png, <prefix>001.png, ..., where <prefix> specifies the sequence name and “.png” indicates that the images [3] are coded using PNG (pronounced “ping”) image compression format [5, 1].

2 What do I have to do?

Sistemas Multimedia - Syllabus

Vicente González Ruiz - Depto Informática - UAL

March 29, 2023

1 About

Sistemas Multimedia (SM) is a compulsory subject of the Master in Technologies and Applications in Computer Science at the UAL (University of Almería).

2 Course Meeting Times

See the current time-table.

3 Scope

SM introduces and develops some of the most used techniques used in image and video coding systems.

4 Main goals

To develop:

  1. The ability to use and develop methodologies, methods, techniques, specific use programs,norms and standards of graphic computing (TI10).
  2. The ability to conceptualize, design, develop and evaluate the human-computer interaction ofproducts, systems, applications and IT services (TI11).
  3. The capacity for the creation and exploitation of virtual environments, and for the creation, management and distribution of multimedia contents (TI12).

5 Methodology and Contents

SM follows the PBL (Project-Based Learning) methodology. The students, helped by the lecturer, develop a project during the classes. This project is VCF, a framework for image and video coding.

The contents of the subject can be found here.

6 Attendance

Currently, the course is organized in 8 lectures of 2 hours/session, during 5 weeks, and it has been developed to be both, virtual and blended training. This means that all the content is available online, but students are expected to regularly attend lectures (at least, remotely), which are mainly practical.
